How It Works

1

Register

After you apply, you will be interviewed by a senior doctors panel. Then you have to go through our training process. After final selection, you will get the Olwel Doctors App which you will have to install on your mobile.

2

You will get notified when we get a patient nearby. You may accept or decline the request.

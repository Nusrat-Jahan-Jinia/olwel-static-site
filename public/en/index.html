Frequently Asked Questions

What is Olwel?
Olwel is a Finland based company. Our aim is to improve the healthcare system in developing countries. We have selected the capital of Bangladesh, Dhaka, as our first operating location. We have studied the healthcare system in Bangladesh and found that the absence of general practitioners is creating a huge load on specialist doctors. We have developed an innovative service platform to tackle this problem. Our platform connects qualified and trained general practitioners with patients so the service can be delivered directly to patients home.
Where is Olwel available?
We provide our service throughout Dhaka now. With the immense amount of support and appreciation that we have received, we would like to expand our services and provide the best healthcare to the people throughout the country.
Who are the doctors?
Our doctors have gone through a rigorous selection process. They have passed their MBBS degree from a reputed medical college and have a verified BMDC registration. They have at least 2 years of experience after passing their internship. They have gone through a selection board made up of senior doctors and specialists. Then they receive special training by our local expert doctors panel, expatriate doctors panel and a formal behavioural training.
What if I have more than one patient?
No problem. Our doctors will treat each patient individually.
How should I pay the doctor?
Our doctor will collect the payment from you at the end of the visit.
